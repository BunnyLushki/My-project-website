Travel to Mysore

So you want to travel to mysore? Well there are lots of good places to visit in Mysore!
For example, Mysore zoo, where you can see lots of animals! Heres a picture of some crocodiles happily sunbathing.

Fun Fact:Crocodiles are cold blooded creatures, unlike us warm blooded creatures.They don't have control of their body temperatures , so they lie in the sun to keep themselves warm. This is called "sunbathing".
